Past Events

Paul Smith, Choose Your Own Adventure

16th June

A successful startup rarely happens by accident; it is the sum of thousands of right choices, usually executed in the right order and nearly always at the right time. The chances of success are slim; the probability of failure, a near-certainty.

How do you improve the odds of success? And can you game the system?

Adam Bird, Transformative Thinking - How the Seedcamp US Trip changed Cronofy

19th May

The Seedcamp US Trip is an assault on the mind, body and senses. In Adam's case it was an amazing opportunity to understand what it's going to take to fully realise the Cronofy opportunity. Adam will share his experiences, conclusions and discovery that 'The Valley' really is another world.

Keith Williams, The journey of how a scientist became an entrepreneur

21st April

Keith Williams describes the seemingly unconnected steps to setting up his own Life Science consultancy business, the hurdles he had to overcome, some of the lessons that he learned building up GxPi and developing software and selling it to software specialist Formpipe for £1.5 million.

Ian Naylor, App Institute

17th March

Ian will give a talk on the journey of App Institute (his company) over the last two years and how they've raised £1 million in funding over various stages (seed, angel, grants and VC). He will talk about the lessons learnt over time and how you can benefit from these too.

Councillor Nick McDonald, Tech Startups and The Nottingham Economy

17th February

Nick is the Cabinet Member for Jobs and Growth at Nottingham City Council. Nick has played a leading role in developing the City’s Economic Growth Plan, and led negotiations for the City on Nottingham’s City Deal.

About Nott Tuesday

Each month we have stimulating speakers giving talks on the challenges of starting and running a technology business, practical guidance on operating a business such as finding funding and exploring future technologies for new opportunities.

The goal of our Meetup is to bring together technology people, startups and companies. Our vision is to bring the Nottingham tech scene together to create great conversations and fascinating insight.

Each year we also run the Tech Startup Awards which celebrates the most promising new tech startup businesses in Nottingham.
