A walkthrough of the evening

Our evening will feature each finalist of the two categories pitching their startup to both the audience and judging panel.

Each finalist gets five minutes to pitch followed by 10 mins Q&A with our judging panel.

Once all pitches have finished the judges will retire to deliberate on their decision and food (in addition to our open bar) will be provided for everybody in attendance.

After time for food to go down and the judges to have made their decision we will announce the winners of each category and prizes will be presented. This will be then followed by networking and discussion on the events of the evening.

The perfect pitch

When entering your startup or (if made a finalist) making your pitch to the judges consider the following key items should be presented:

  • Business overview
  • Market information i.e. opportunity and competition
  • Management team
  • Key achievements
  • Growth strategy (management needed, investment & exit strategy)

The trick is to keep the application and pitch concise. You have five minutes to impress, make the best of them.

About Nott Tuesday

Each month we have stimulating speakers giving talks on the challenges of starting and running a technology business, practical guidance on operating a business such as finding funding and exploring future technologies for new opportunities.

The goal of our Meetup is to bring together technology people, startups and companies. Our vision is to bring the Nottingham tech scene together to create great conversations and fascinating insight.

Each year we also run the Tech Startup Awards which celebrates the most promising new tech startup businesses in Nottingham.
